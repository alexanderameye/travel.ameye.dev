Overview

  • Destination: Japan
  • Dates: 29/05/2025 - 15/06/2025

Day 1: 29/05/2025

  • Flight: Arrive at Tokyio International Haneda
  • Accommodation: Shibuya Hotel
  • Activities:
    • Explore Shibuya Crossing, one of the busiest pedestrian intersections in the world
    • Visit the Hachiko Statue at Shibuya Station
    • Enjoy dinner at a sushi bar in Shinjuku

Day 2: 30/05/2025

  • Morning:
    • Visit the Meiji Shrine in Harajuku
    • Stroll around Takeshita Street for quirky shops and fashion
  • Afternoon:
    • Explore Asakusa Temple and the famous Senso-ji Temple
    • Walk along the Sumida River and take a boat cruise
  • Evening:
    • Visit Tokyo Skytree for panoramic views of the city

Day 3: 31/05/2025

  • Morning: Take the train to Nikko (2 hours)
  • Activities:
    • Visit the Toshogu Shrine, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
    • Explore Lake Chuzenji and Kegon Falls
  • Evening: Return to Tokyo and enjoy dinner at a ramen restaurant

Day 4: Travel to Kyoto

  • Morning: Take the Shinkansen (bullet train) to Kyoto (2.5 hours)
  • Accommodation: Kyoto Ryokan (traditional Japanese inn)
  • Afternoon:
    • Visit the Fushimi Inari Shrine with its iconic red torii gates
    • Explore the Kiyomizu-dera Temple and the historic Higashiyama district
  • Evening:
    • Dinner in Gion district, known for its geisha culture

Day 5: Kyoto Sightseeing

  • Morning:
    • Explore the Golden Pavilion (Kinkaku-ji)
    • Visit the Ryoan-ji Temple and its famous Zen rock garden
  • Afternoon:
    • Stroll through the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
    • Visit the Tenryu-ji Temple and its beautiful gardens
  • Evening:
    • Enjoy a traditional tea ceremony in one of Kyoto's teahouses

Day 6: Day Trip to Nara

  • Morning: Take the train to Nara (1 hour)
  • Activities:
    • Visit the Todai-ji Temple to see the giant Buddha statue
    • Stroll through Nara Park and feed the friendly deer
  • Afternoon:
    • Explore the Kasuga Taisha Shrine and its forested path
  • Evening: Return to Kyoto for a quiet evening

Day 7: Travel to Osaka

  • Morning: Take the train to Osaka (30 minutes)
  • Accommodation: Osaka Hotel
  • Activities:
    • Explore Osaka Castle and its beautiful park
    • Visit the Umeda Sky Building for city views
  • Evening:
    • Experience the lively atmosphere of Dotonbori, famous for its neon lights and street food

Day 8: Osaka Sightseeing

  • Morning:
    • Visit Universal Studios Japan for a day of theme park fun
  • Afternoon:
    • Visit the Osaka Aquarium (Kaiyukan) and the Tempozan Ferris Wheel
  • Evening:
    • Explore the Shinsaibashi Shopping Arcade

Day 9: Day Trip to Hiroshima

  • Morning: Take the Shinkansen to Hiroshima (2.5 hours)
  • Activities:
    • Visit the Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park and Atomic Bomb Dome
    • Take a ferry to Miyajima Island and see the iconic Itsukushima Shrine
  • Evening: Return to Osaka for the night

Day 10: Departure

  • Morning:
    • Last-minute shopping in Osaka's Namba District
    • Relax at a local café
  • Afternoon:
    • Head to Kansai International Airport for your flight home

Notes

  • Weather: Spring (April) in Japan is mild, with cherry blossoms in full bloom. Pack light layers and an umbrella.
  • Language: Japanese is the main language, but most people understand basic English in tourist areas.
  • Safety: Japan is one of the safest countries for travelers.
